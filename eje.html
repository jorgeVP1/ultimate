Flint Lockwood Diatonic Super Mutating Dynamic Food Replicator

The Flint Lockwood Diatonic Super Mutating Dynamic Food Replicator,
abbreviated as FLDSMDFR, is an advanced device invented by Flint Lockwood.
Capable of turning water molecules into food, its purpose is to expand
the diets of the citizens of Swallow Falls, who were forced to eat the town's
sardine-based products. The FLDSMDFR is the secondary antagonist of Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs,
and the overarching protagonist of Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs 2.
